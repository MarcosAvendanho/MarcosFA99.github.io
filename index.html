ABOUT

marcos fernández avendaño currently known as Marcosfa is a Spanish biketrial rider,
He was born in 2000 and at the young age of 5, he started with the Trial.
beginning to control the accelerator of his father's motorcycle (Gas Gas 280cc 2005), in 2008 he was runner-up Gallego.
Later he began training by bike when he was given a biketrial bike for his first communion in 2008 (Monty 219)
Since that time he has not stopped training to date and continues to set new goals every day.
"I don't know if I will become world champion, but if I have reached the middle of the road with 4 podiums from Spain, I will not stop now." he comments in one of his interviews.
Marcos has made numerous exhibitions at GALIEXPO, one of the most important motor fairs in Galicia, filling ships of up to 15,000 people
Currently Sponsored by Bici O Con and TxokoSurfWear and in the club + Enduro for which it was selected for the enduro World Series, he still has a lot of career as an athlete ahead
